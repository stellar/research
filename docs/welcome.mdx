# Research at the Stellar Development Foundation

The [Stellar Development Foundation](https://stellar.org) supports technological and scientific research through its in-house research team and its academic grants program.

If you are interested, please reach out to [giuliano@stellar.org](mailto:giuliano@stellar.org).
